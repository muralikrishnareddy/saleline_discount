Sale Line Discount based on Role

Control Discount on Sales Line based on User Sales Role and role based discount configuration for each Product.

To show the discounts in Sale order line, install "Allow setting a discount on the sales order lines" from Sales settings.

Company level shall be configured to give for each Sales Role discount percent allowed.

Company level configured role based discounts shall be shown by default for each product, based on product we shall be allowed to change or delete the discount for the Role.
